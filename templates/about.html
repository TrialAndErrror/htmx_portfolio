About Trial and Errror

Trial and Errror is a creative brand focused on Web Development, Technical Consulting, and Video Content Development and Production.

Created by Wade Green Trial and Errror has been building quality web applications and websites since 2019. Trial and Errror also provides valuable insights and advice around Web Development and Application building.

Founded on the idea of "Everyone Makes Mistakes", Trial and Errror is focused on developing quality applications as well as educating technology users about best practices around use and growth of their applications.

Video Content

As part of its efforts to educate users, Trial and Errror produces YouTube videos on topics centered around Programming, Web Development, and Technology in general.

Trial and Errror uses a combination of technical explanations, risk evaluations, and fundamentals of programming to help empower users to make the right technology choices for themselves.

If you're curious about new technology, or you want to learn more about the hardware and software you already use, head over to the YouTube Channel to see more from Trial and Errror!
